# 1.7 极坐标

$\rho=4\sin\theta$ 计算圆  

$$
\begin{align}
&\rho^2=4\rho\sin\theta\\
\because\,&x=\rho\cos\theta,y=\rho\sin\theta,x^2+y^2=\rho^2\\
\therefore\,&x^2+y^2=4y\\
\,&x^2+(y-2)^2=4
\end{align}
$$
